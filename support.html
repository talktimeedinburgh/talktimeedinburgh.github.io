SUPPORT US

TalkTime Edinburgh is a Scottish registered charity which is funded entirely by donation and run by volunteers.

The service we offer is limited by the amount of funding we receive.

If you would like to get involved in fundraising for us, whether it be through having a fancy dress day at your school, running a bake sale, or even doing a sponsored sky dive, we would love to hear from you.

If you work for a company with a corporate giving scheme that would like to support a new local charity working with young disabled people, please consider us in your plans.

Thank you.

Seonaid Cooke
Founder and Trustee
